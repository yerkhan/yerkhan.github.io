Who am I?

Everyone in this life has a unique life-story and I am not an exception. My life-journey can be shortly described as "from engineering - through education - towards therapy".

Having a background in CS and engineering (both BCs and MSc), I have work experience in education for more than 15 years and now I'm at the finish line of my doctorate degree (PhD).

As a Rapid Transformational Therapist, I help people with issues such as fear, phobias, stress, anxiety, self-confidence, self-esteem, eating disorder and addiction. I am a member of the EAHP . I also speak Russian, Turkish and Kazakh. Mostly my RTT sessions are in English, however, I can do sessions in any of the mentioned languages. If you think I can help you, let me know by contacting me, we can arrange a free discovery call.
